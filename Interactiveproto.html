Wireframe Prototype Design Rationale

Our application has an overall youthful and semi-minimalistic design theme. We tried to make the design cheerful and interactive for our target users, who are typically college students. However, in order to prevent the application from causing too many distractions, we also tried to make our design semi-minimalistic. We avoided adding unnecessary or redundant features, and we tried to keep each screen as clean and organized as possible.

InVision Prototype Design Rationale

Color Scheme



  • We chose the color green as our main color scheme because green can usually symbolize or bring comfort.
  • We wanted to make our application a comforting and relaxing place for users, who are usually college students seeking to use our app to focus and interact with others.
  • Green can also signify friendliness and peace, which also complies with the goal of application helping anxious college students focus and establish a peaceful community.
  • We also used a more monochromatic color scheme by using different shades of green. This allows us to keep our design more consistent and to create internal consistency, which can increase our application's learnability.
  • We also have the option of customizable backgrounds that provides the users with more freedom and customization so that users have the option to personalize their experience by either uploading their own backgrounds or choosing their own color schemes.

Graphic Design

  • This design was made keeping young audiences in mind. We incorporated a social media aspect in our design in order to keep our audience engaged.
  • Most of the tasks can be done with a few clicks with this design. There aren’t too many extra, complicated designs incorporated, which can increase the efficiency and learnability of the design.
  • The design also utilizes a variety of icons and interactive features. Users also have the ability to personalize their experiences.
  • There is also internal color consistency for each course, which can also contribute to the memorability of the application by making it easier for users to identify features by colors.
  • Grid system with invisible grids for organization yet no visual cluster
  • Metaphorical icons were used to increase learnability.

Font/Typography

  • Our application uses the font family Montserrat as the main font. Montserrat has a light font weight, bringing lightness and creating a sense of cleanliness.
  • The fact that this font family is simple and clean looking can make the text of the application easier for users to read.
  • It can also prevent clustering of design and text throughout the page, so users will not feel overwhelmed with content on their screen.
  • Moreover, this Montserrat font can make the design look organized since it is simple and has high readability. We also chose font colors that have some contrast with the background to make the texts stand out.
  • We also used grids as the main element of our application design to create a sense of organization and to make the application easy for users to navigate.
  • For external consistency, we used font-color blue and underline to indicate clickable links (in zoom directory section).
