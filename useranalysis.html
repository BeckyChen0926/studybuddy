User Analysis

Takeaways from User Interviews

1. Interviewees reported that online courses are isolating, and it is often difficult to study by themselves at home.

2. The aspect of collaborative work is lacking for online classes.

3. Interviewees dislike how distant everyone feels when taking online classes.

4. It is often more difficult to create a community online.

5. It is harder for students to focus when they are studying alone at home, and they may often feel less motivated.
